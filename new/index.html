Biography

Dr. Arnaout is a Simulation Tools Developer & Analyst in the Research & Development team at Kiva Systems (Amazon.com subsidiary). Dr. Arnaout received his Ph.D. in Engineering Management (concentration on simulation) at Old Dominion University, Norfolk, VA.
